The Raiden Shogun (Japanese: 雷らい電でん将しょう軍ぐん Raiden Shougun) is a playable Electro character in Genshin Impact. The Raiden Shogun is comprised of two beings in one body: Ei, the current Electro Archon of Inazuma; and the Shogun, the puppet created by Ei to act as the ruler of Inazuma in her stead.

Neuvillette is an upcoming playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact. Neuvillette is the Iudex of Fontaine, and the leader of the Marechaussee Phantom. While Neuvillette upholds the rules of the court with utmost reverence and frequently keeps Furina's flamboyancy in check, he is quite aloof when dealing with human emotions and often distances himself from the public eye.
